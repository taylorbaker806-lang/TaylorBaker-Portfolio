Who I Am

I'm Taylor Baker, a Business Management student at Brigham Young University with a passion for leadership and problem-solving. My journey has taken me from the classrooms of Frenship High School, where I graduated in the top 4% of my class, to serving as a missionary in the Salt Lake City East Mission, and now to pursuing my degree at BYU.

Throughout my experiences, I've developed a strong foundation in team leadership, cross-cultural communication, and operational management. Whether I was supervising a team of 15-20 missionaries, installing commercial playground systems, or providing exceptional customer service at Chick-fil-A, I've consistently demonstrated my ability to adapt, lead, and deliver results.

What sets me apart is my intrinsic motivation and ability to connect with people from all walks of life. My missionary service taught me to communicate effectively in English and Swahili, work with diverse communities, and lead with empathy and purpose. These experiences, combined with my business acumen and quick learning ability, position me to make meaningful contributions in any professional environment.

I'm currently seeking opportunities to apply my skills in a dynamic team setting where I can continue to grow, contribute to organizational success, and make a positive impact.
